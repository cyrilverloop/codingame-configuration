- 100g Prawns
- 5cl Oil
In a large saucepan, brown the shelled prawns in oil (2 to 3 minutes).
- 40cl Coconut milk
- 1g turmeric
- 1g curry
- 4g Bay leaves
When cooked, add the coconut milk, turmeric, curry and bay leaves. Salt and pepper.
Cook over medium heat until the coconut milk is very yellow (7 to 10 min).
Remove the bay leaves and serve immediately with white rice.
